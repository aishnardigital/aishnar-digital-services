About AishNar Digital

We're on a mission to make digital marketing accessible and effective for local businesses in Rajasthan.

Our Story

Founded on July 1st, 2025 by Narender Kumar and Aishwary, AishNar Digital was born from a simple observation: local businesses in Suratgarh were struggling to establish their digital presence despite having amazing products and services.

We noticed that most digital agencies were either too expensive or focused on big clients, leaving small businesses behind. That's when we decided to create something different—a digital partner that truly understands local needs and budgets.

Our Mission

To empower local shopkeepers, food stalls, and small businesses in Suratgarh and beyond with affordable, smart digital solutions that drive real growth and help them compete in the digital age.
